Privacy Policy

App: IFC Clash Detector

Last updated: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

IFC Clash Detector is designed to run locally on your device. We do not require account creation or sign-in.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location through the app.

IFC model files you import are processed on-device for clash detection and reporting.

3. Data Storage

Project/session data is stored locally on your device. You control your files and exports (for example PDF and BCF).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Data is only shared when you explicitly export or share files using Apple system share/save tools.

5. Network Use

The app may use network access for app functionality (for example loading web resources used by the viewer) and opening external links. This policy does not cover third-party websites you choose to open.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • You can delete local app data by removing the app and its documents from your device.
  • You can control file access permissions in iOS/macOS Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
